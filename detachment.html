the problem

your brain
has a loop.

every bad habit follows the exact same pattern. boredom leads to a trigger. the trigger creates an urge. the urge leads to the behavior. temporary relief. then the loop repeats — stronger than before.

01

trigger

something sets it off. boredom. loneliness. stress. a notification. seeing their name. it doesn't have to be big — the brain is always looking for an excuse.

02

urge

your brain demands dopamine. it says: open instagram. check their profile. have a drink. send that message. it feels urgent. it isn't.

03

action

you do it before you even realize what happened. it wasn't a decision. it was the loop completing itself automatically.

04

reinforcement

brief relief. then shame. then the loop is stronger. your brain learned the behavior works. it'll ask for it again sooner next time.

⚡
detachment interrupts this. it doesn't ask you to be strong in the moment of weakness. it removes the moment entirely — by stepping in before the action happens.

the architecture

five systems.
one goal.

each system does one thing: create enough distance between the urge and the action that the urge dies on its own.

01
🔥
streak system

counts how many days you've been clean. four separate streaks if you're fighting four battles. every milestone is proof the system is working — not willpower, the system.

02
⚠️
reality vault

you write one sentence on day one. why you actually want to stop. the app saves it. every time the shield fires, the first thing you see is that sentence — in your own words.

03
🛡️
trigger shield

detects when you're about to relapse — by tracking which apps you open, where you are, and what time it is. then takes over your screen for 60 seconds. not a notification. a full block.

04
📓
journal log

write or talk. just vent out loud — the ai listens, transcribes what you said, and reflects back what it actually heard underneath the words. the more you log, the smarter the detection gets.

05
🎭
feel

type what you want to feel — "i want to cry" or "i need to feel strong". ai reads your emotional state and finds youtube content that matches it exactly. no playlists to browse. just one input.
